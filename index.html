GISP Lab

Welcome to the website of GISP (Group of Intelligent Signal Processing, 智能信号处理组) Lab! GISP is a research laboratory founded by Dr. Jian Guan at Harbin Engineering University (HEU). We focus on audio signal processing, remote sensing image interpretation and artificial intelligence. Our main interests are the basic theoretical and applied research of signal processing driven by artificial intelligence technologies, and our research includes:

  • Acoustic scene and event classification and detection;
  • Acoustic channel modeling;
  • Blind signal estimation;
  • Speech enhancement;
  • Remote sensing target detection/recognition;
  • Sparse representation methods and applications;
  • Deep learning theory and applications;
